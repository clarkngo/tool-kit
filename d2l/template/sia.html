Part 1 - Introduce Yourself

The purpose of this activity is to get to know each other and help you better collaborate. Please include the following in your introduction:

  • Introduce yourself and welcome your classmates.
  • Tell us why have you selected this course and what you are expecting to learn.
  • Please share any information about:
    • Your undergraduate or graduate degrees
    • Your current professional position if you are working
    • Your work experiences
    • Your experiences in programming
    • Your professional interests
  • Post a photo of yourself that you use or will use in your Brightspace profile.

To submit your introduction, select Start a New Thread and then type a subject and your introduction. When you are ready to submit your post, select Post.
